OVERVIEW OF THE CONVOCATION OF THE EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CENTRE(EASTC)

The convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition with the academic institutions of the gathering together academicians,students,alumni,benefactors,and industry professionals as stakeholder of the universty community.It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching research and professional development

This year's convocation has selected "The emerging data science and digital innovation ecoystem of tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of thi year's 18November African Statistical DAY. The theme states"Strengthening data systems by modernizing production and use of agriculture statistics: informing policies with the view to improving resillence in agriculture,nutrition and food security in Africa".

African statistics Day is celebrated ach years.The commration aims among others, to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development . This year's convocation aims to ucover the trends so far government support and emerging efforts in the public and private sectors that combine data use and technology in solutions that can be scaled to adress challenges within Tanzania's and Africa's agriculture and other sector
